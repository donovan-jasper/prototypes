# Bonsai Buddy

> A mobile-first plant care app that gamifies and socializes plant ownership, blending journaling with community features and expert guidance.

**Gap:** Existing apps lack social features, gamification, or expert-curated care guides. Most are either overly technical or too generic for casual users.

## Features

- Anxious first-time plant owners** who kill everything and want to break the cycle
- Apartment dwellers** seeking to bring nature indoors without a yard
- Parents teaching kids responsibility** through plant care (gateway to pet ownership)
- Mental health seekers** using plant care as a mindfulness practice
