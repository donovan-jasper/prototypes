# PlantPulse

A mobile-first plant care app that gamifies and socializes plant ownership, blending journaling with community features and expert guidance.

**Who it's for:** Plant enthusiasts (novices to experts), urban gardeners, and homeowners with limited green thumbs who want to track and improve their plant care skills.

**Gap:** Existing apps lack social features, gamification, or expert-curated care guides. Most are either overly technical or too generic for casual users.

**Monetization:** Freemium model with premium features like expert advice, ad-free experience, and community access. One-time purchase for offline plant databases.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 6/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires strong UX for plant care workflows and community engagement mechanics.**

npx expo start
