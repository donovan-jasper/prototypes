# Support Sentinel

A mobile app that instantly alerts users when their customer service requests are delayed, with real-time tracking and automated follow-ups for any business using online support platforms.

**Who it's for:** Small business owners, e-commerce sellers, and customers of any service with online support (e.g., Uber, Airbnb, SaaS companies).

**Gap:** Most support tracking apps are desktop-focused or lack proactive notifications. Users waste time checking statuses manually.

**Monetization:** Freemium model: Free basic tracking, $9.99/month for real-time alerts and automated follow-ups. Businesses pay $49/month for team-wide support monitoring.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 8/10 | Difficulty: Medium—requires integration with multiple support APIs and real-time notification infrastructure.**
