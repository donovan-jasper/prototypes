# VoxCrew

A voice-first, AI-powered communication and workflow hub for field workers across industries, enabling hands-free collaboration, task updates, and knowledge retrieval without typing or app switching.

**Who it's for:** Field workers in construction, logistics, manufacturing, and service industries (e.g., plumbers, electricians, delivery drivers, warehouse staff) who need to update systems, communicate, and access AI assistance while mobile.

**Gap:** Existing tools force field workers to type, switch apps, or rely on non-integrated voice assistants (e.g., Alexa), creating friction in workflows and knowledge gaps.

**Monetization:** Subscription model ($15–$30/month per user) with tiered pricing for teams, plus enterprise integrations. Freemium for small teams with limited voice features.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium—requires robust voice-to-text, AI training, and seamless integrations, but the problem is clear and underserved.**
