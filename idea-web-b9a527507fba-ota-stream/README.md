# OTA Stream Spec

A mobile app that aggregates and streams local TV channels, sports, and live events with personalized alerts and social sharing features.

**Who it's for:** Sports fans, local news followers, and event attendees who want to watch live content on their phones without cable or streaming services.

**Gap:** Most apps lack real-time local channel aggregation, personalized event alerts, or seamless social sharing of broadcasts.

**Monetization:** Freemium model with premium features like ad-free streaming, exclusive local channels, and social sharing unlocks at $4.99/month.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires partnerships with local broadcasters and robust content aggregation.**
