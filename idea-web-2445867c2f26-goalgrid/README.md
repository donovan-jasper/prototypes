# GoalGrid Spec

A habit-building and productivity app that gamifies goal-setting with social accountability and AI-driven motivation for personal and professional growth

**Who it's for:** Busy professionals (25-45), students, and parents who struggle with time management and motivation, as well as anyone seeking to build consistent habits (e.g., fitness, learning, financial discipline)

**Gap:** Most apps lack AI-driven personalized motivation or social accountability features. Many are too rigid or lack visual progress tracking.

**Monetization:** Freemium model with premium features like AI coaching ($9.99/month) and social accountability groups ($4.99/month). One-time premium version for power users.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires strong UX for habit formation and AI integration, but clear demand exists**
