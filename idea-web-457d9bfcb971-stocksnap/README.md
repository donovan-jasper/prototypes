# FinchApp

A mobile app that simplifies financial literacy by providing real-time stock research, company insights, and investment education for non-technical users.

**Who it's for:** Retirees (65+), young adults (18-35) with no prior investing experience, and professionals in finance-adjacent fields (e.g., HR, real estate).

**Gap:** Most apps overwhelm beginners with complex data; few offer simplified explanations, personalized recommendations, or gamified learning.

**Monetization:** Freemium model: Free tier with basic research; premium ($9.99/mo) for alerts, advanced analytics, and a curated newsletter.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 8/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires strong data partnerships and UX for non-finance users.**
