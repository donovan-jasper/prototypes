# VocaVault

A mobile-first, AI-powered language learning app that uses spaced repetition to teach the most frequent words in any language, with personalized retention tracking and cross-platform sync.

**Who it's for:** Language learners (ESL students, travelers, professionals), polyglots, and anyone interested in quick vocabulary acquisition. Also, commuters, students, and professionals who want bite-sized learning.

**Gap:** Most apps focus on grammar or long-term learning. Few prioritize ultra-frequent words for immediate practical use. None combine FSRS with AI-driven word selection and real-world context.

**Monetization:** One-time purchase ($1.99) for the core app, with optional premium features like AI-powered word selection, real-time translation, and offline voice recognition ($4.99/mo).

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 5/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires strong UX for spaced repetition, but the concept is proven. Scaling language support and AI features will be challenging.**
