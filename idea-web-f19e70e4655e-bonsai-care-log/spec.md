# Verdant — Universal Plant Care Companion

## One-line pitch
Track every plant's journey from seedling to thriving greenery with photo timelines, smart care reminders, and a community that grows with you.

## Expanded vision

**Core audience:** Plant parents aged 25-45 who treat their houseplants like pets — they want to document growth, prevent neglect, and share their green thumb wins.

**Broader reach:**
- **Apartment dwellers** who need to maximize limited space with healthy plants
- **Remote workers** building biophilic home offices and tracking which plants thrive in their environment
