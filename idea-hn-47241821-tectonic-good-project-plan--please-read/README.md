# SafeCircle

A mobile-first platform empowering vulnerable populations with AI-driven, location-based tools for safety, legal rights, and community support, delivered instantly via SMS or app.

**Who it's for:** Homeless individuals, low-income families, refugees, domestic violence survivors, and disaster victims—groups with limited digital access but urgent needs.

**Gap:** Most solutions are web-based, require high literacy, or lack AI personalization. Many don't integrate location, legal, or emergency services in one app.

**Monetization:** Freemium: Core tools free, premium for advanced features (e.g., legal document generation, ad-free experience) or one-time $5 for critical services (e.g., eviction letters).

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 6/10 | Difficulty: Medium—requires partnerships with shelters, legal experts, and pharmacies, but leverages existing APIs and AI models.**
