# PlayPals Mobile App Specification

A hyper-local social app that connects people to spontaneous, interest-based activities (sports, games, fitness, hobbies) in real-time, leveraging mobile's native advantages for discovery and coordination.

**Who it's for:** Young adults (18-35) who want to stay active, meet new people, or explore their city without long-term commitments; parents looking for structured kid-friendly activities; fitness enthusiasts seeking structured workouts.

**Gap:** Existing apps either lack real-time matching, require long-term commitments, or focus too narrowly on specific sports. PlayPals could offer a frictionless, gamified way to find and join spontaneous activities.

**Monetization:** Freemium with premium features like ad-free browsing, exclusive event access, or virtual rewards for activity participation. Sponsorships from local gyms, sports brands, or city tourism boards could fund the free tier.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires strong community-building, real-time matching algorithms, and local partnerships.**
