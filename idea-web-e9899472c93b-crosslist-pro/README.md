# ListSync

A mobile app that helps small businesses and e-commerce sellers automate product listings across platforms like Amazon, eBay, and Shopify with real-time inventory sync and pricing optimization.

**Who it's for:** Small business owners, e-commerce sellers, and dropshippers who sell on multiple platforms but lack time or expertise to manage listings manually.

**Gap:** Existing tools are either platform-specific, lack automation, or require technical setup. A unified mobile app with AI-driven pricing suggestions and seamless sync would fill this gap.

**Monetization:** Freemium model with a $9.99/month premium tier for advanced features like AI pricing optimization and bulk listing tools. One-time $99 lifetime license for small sellers.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 4/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires API integrations with e-commerce platforms and AI/ML for pricing optimization.**
