# ConnectCircle

A mobile app that gamifies and simplifies relationship maintenance for all adults, blending reminders, suggestions, and social analytics into a habit-forming experience.

**Who it's for:** Adults aged 25-55 who have 5+ friends but struggle to maintain meaningful connections (e.g., busy professionals, parents, remote workers).

**Gap:** Existing apps focus on either tracking or gamification but lack a seamless blend of both. Most also lack deep personalization or social analytics.

**Monetization:** Freemium with a $4.99/month premium tier unlocking advanced analytics, AI-generated suggestions, and ad-free experience. One-time $29.99 for a 'Relationship Boost' pack.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires UX polish to avoid feeling like a chore, and data privacy concerns around relationship insights.**
