# Friendship Keeper App Spec

A relationship maintenance platform that helps people nurture all their personal connections through automated touchpoints, shared experiences, and relationship health tracking.

**Who it's for:** Adults 25-45 who are busy professionals with demanding careers but want to maintain meaningful relationships; parents juggling family and work who struggle to stay connected with friends; people who move frequently for work and lose touch with important relationships; individuals coming out of major life transitions who want to rebuild social connections.

**Gap:** Existing apps are either too generic (just reminder apps), too narrow (only romantic relationships), or too manual (require constant input). There's no comprehensive solution that intelligently suggests appropriate connection methods based on relationship type, history, and preferences while making it effortless to maintain multiple relationships simultaneously.

**Monetization:** Freemium model: Free tier allows 3-5 relationships with basic reminders; Premium at $4.99/month includes unlimited relationships, smart suggestions, shared activity planning, relationship analytics, and cross-platform sync. People would pay because maintaining relationships is emotionally valuable and the time-saving aspect is worth the cost for busy professionals.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 6/10 | Difficulty: Medium - Requires thoughtful UX to avoid feeling robotic in relationships, integration with contacts/calendar systems, and AI-driven personalization without being creepy. Technical complexity is moderate but success depends heavily on getting the human psychology right.**
