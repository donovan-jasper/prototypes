# RoutineRhythm Spec

A dynamic personal assistant app that intelligently adapts a user's entire life — tasks, habits, appointments, and self-care — to their constantly changing availability and commitments, ensuring productivity and well-being.

**Who it's for:** Primarily shift workers (nurses, retail, hospitality, gig economy drivers, pilots, first responders), students with variable class schedules, parents with co-parenting arrangements, freelancers, and anyone whose core work or life commitments fluctuate. Secondarily, individuals seeking better work-life balance, those struggling with consistent routines due to ADHD or life changes, and people overwhelmed by managing multiple, unpredictable commitments.

**Gap:** Existing solutions are largely static or siloed. Calendar apps manage appointments, to-do apps manage tasks, and habit trackers manage habits, but none intelligently orchestrate *all* these elements and dynamically adapt them to a *constantly changing core schedule* (like variable work shifts). There's a significant lack of a holistic, proactive, and adaptive personal assistant that reduces the mental load for individuals with non-standard or unpredictable routines.

**Monetization:** Freemium with a premium subscription model. The free tier would offer basic task/habit management and manual schedule input. The premium tier ($4.99/month or $49.99/year) would unlock intelligent features like automatic work schedule syncing (e.g., via calendar APIs), AI-driven routine optimization and task suggestions, unlimited routines/habits, advanced analytics, cross-device sync, and customizable smart notifications. People would pay for the significant time savings, reduced stress, and improved work-life balance that a truly adaptive system provides.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium**
