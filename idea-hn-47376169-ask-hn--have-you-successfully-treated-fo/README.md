# PosturePal

A mobile app that gamifies posture correction exercises with AI feedback and personalized coaching for desk workers and remote professionals

**Who it's for:** Remote workers, office professionals, students, and anyone who spends long hours in front of screens (ages 25-55)

**Gap:** Most apps lack AI-driven personalization, real-time feedback, or integration with ergonomic tools like standing desks

**Monetization:** Freemium model with premium features like AI coaching ($9.99/month) and hardware integrations (e.g., smart desk sync)

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 6/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires motion sensor expertise and medical validation for posture correction**
