# BrainVault

BrainVault is a sleek, all-in-one digital brain for capturing, organizing, and recalling everything you need.

## Features

- **Instant Capture**: Save notes, links, images, and voice memos from any app.
- **Smart Channels**: Organize your content with pre-built or customizable channels.
- **Private & Secure**: End-to-end encryption with biometric or passcode protection.
- **Quick Recall**: Search your content using voice or contextual suggestions.
- **Cross-Device Sync**: Keep your content in sync across all your devices.
