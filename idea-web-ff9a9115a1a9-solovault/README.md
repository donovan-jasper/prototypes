# SoloVault → **BrainVault**

A personal digital assistant and "second brain" app that allows users to effortlessly capture, organize, and retrieve any type of personal information (notes, links, images, voice memos) across all their devices, using an intuitive chat-like interface for quick input and categorized storage.

**Who it's for:** Knowledge workers, students, creatives, and anyone who frequently consumes and needs to organize diverse information (links, articles, images, notes) but finds existing tools either too complex or too limited. It's for individuals seeking a simple, private digital journal or a personal system to manage information overload without cluttering other apps.

**Gap:** Existing solutions often force a trade-off between simplicity of input and robust organization, or they specialize in one content type (e.g., just links, just notes). There's a gap for a truly intuitive, multi-device 'second brain' that combines chat-like ease of capture for all content types with flexible, channel-based organization, without the complexity of advanced knowledge management systems.

**Monetization:** Freemium model: A free tier offers basic storage (e.g., 1GB or 500 items/month) and limited groups (e.g., 3). A premium subscription ($4.99/month or $49.99/year) unlocks unlimited storage, unlimited groups, advanced AI-powered search, OCR for images, voice memo transcription, custom themes, and priority support. Users would pay for the peace of mind of unlimited capacity, powerful organization features that save time, and the assurance of a robust, private system for their most valuable personal information.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 5/10 | Difficulty: Medium**
