# JustSwipe → SwipeClear

A mobile app that helps users declutter and organize digital life beyond just photos/videos, including messages, notifications, and app clutter.

**Who it's for:** Overworked professionals (25-45), busy parents, and digital minimalists who struggle with information overload but lack time for deep organization.

**Gap:** Existing apps either focus on storage cleanup or require manual tagging. JustSwipe offers a frictionless, gesture-driven approach for real-time organization.

**Monetization:** Freemium with a $4.99/month premium tier for advanced features like AI-powered sorting and cross-device sync.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 8/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires smooth UX for gesture-based organization and cross-platform integration.**
