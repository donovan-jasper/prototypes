# FinFlow

A mobile app that combines budgeting, investment tracking, and financial insights in a single, privacy-focused tool for financially literate users who want deeper control over their money

**Who it's for:** Young professionals (25-40), side hustlers, and financially independent individuals who track investments (stocks, crypto, real estate) alongside spending, value data privacy, and want a unified dashboard for all financial activity

**Gap:** Most mobile finance apps either focus only on budgeting or investing separately, lack rich visual reports, or require bank sync (raising privacy concerns). Finzen fills this gap with a privacy-first approach and integrated insights.

**Monetization:** Freemium model: Free tier with basic budgeting and portfolio tracking; premium tier ($9.99/month) unlocks advanced reports, multi-currency support, and priority support. Users pay for convenience and deeper financial insights.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium — Requires strong UX for financial data, secure backend infrastructure, and competitive differentiation from established players.**
