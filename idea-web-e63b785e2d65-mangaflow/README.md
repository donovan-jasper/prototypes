# PageTurn

A premium, cross-platform manga reader app that delivers a seamless, ad-free reading experience with advanced library management and support for self-hosted content, appealing to manga enthusiasts and collectors.

**Who it's for:** Manga fans (ages 13-35), collectors, and readers who prefer high-quality, offline-capable apps over web-based or sideloaded solutions. Also, self-hosted users who want a polished interface for their personal manga collections.

**Gap:** Most manga apps are either ad-supported, lack offline features, or have clunky UIs. Many self-hosted users rely on outdated or buggy apps. MangaFlow could offer a polished, premium alternative with better performance and organization tools.

**Monetization:** Premium subscription ($4.99/month) with a free trial, offering ad-free reading, cloud sync, and priority support. One-time purchase ($29.99) for lifetime access to core features.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 6/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires strong backend for self-hosted support and a polished UI, but the niche (premium manga readers) is underserved.**
