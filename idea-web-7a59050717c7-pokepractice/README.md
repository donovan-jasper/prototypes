# StrikeZone

A mobile app for training precision and timing in real-world scenarios (e.g., sports, gaming, fitness) with customizable challenges and performance analytics.

**Who it's for:** Athletes, gamers, fitness enthusiasts, and anyone needing precision training (e.g., archery, basketball, VR gaming).

**Gap:** Most training apps focus on fitness or sports; none combine AR/VR with real-time analytics for precision tasks.

**Monetization:** Freemium with premium AR/VR scenarios ($4.99/month) and one-time purchases for pro training modules ($29.99).

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires motion sensor integration and AR/VR development expertise.**
