# ZenSprint

A mobile app that gamifies productivity with voice coaching, rewards, and social accountability to help users stay focused on work or study tasks.

**Who it's for:** Students (college/grad school), remote workers, parents with kids at home, and anyone struggling with procrastination or time management.

**Gap:** Most apps lack voice coaching, social features, or personalized rewards. Many are too rigid (e.g., strict Pomodoro timers).

**Monetization:** Freemium with premium voice packs ($4.99/mo), custom rewards ($9.99/mo), and a 'FocusFlow Pro' tier ($19.99/mo) with social accountability and analytics.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 4/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires voice synthesis, gamification design, and user retention strategies.**
