# CourseKit

A mobile-first platform for creators to build, sell, and distribute written courses and learning content on the go, with seamless integration of existing writing (Substack, PDFs, Markdown) and AI-assisted course creation tools.

**Who it's for:** Non-technical creators (bloggers, journalists, educators, coaches) who write newsletters, articles, or guides but lack the time or tools to structure them into paid courses. Also, lifelong learners who prefer text-based learning over video.

**Gap:** Most course platforms force video creation, and none optimize for the mobile workflow of writers who already have content in Substack, PDFs, or Markdown. Existing apps lack AI-assisted course structuring or seamless content import.

**Monetization:** Freemium model: Free basic course creation with limited features; $9.99/month for unlimited courses, advanced analytics, and 85% revenue share. One-time $299 for premium templates and priority support.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires strong UX for mobile course creation, AI integration, and seamless content imports, but the niche audience and clear pain points make it viable.**
