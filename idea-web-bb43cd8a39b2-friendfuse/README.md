# FriendSpark

A mobile app that gamifies social connection by rewarding consistent engagement with friends through challenges, streaks, and shared experiences

**Who it's for:** Young professionals (25-40), remote workers, and parents juggling multiple friendships who struggle to maintain deep connections due to busy schedules.

**Gap:** Existing apps lack gamification, streaks, or incentives to maintain friendships. Most focus on new connections rather than existing ones.

**Monetization:** Freemium with premium features like advanced analytics, custom challenges, and ad-free experiences. Users pay $5/month for a 'Friendship Pro' tier.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 6/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires strong UX for social engagement and retention, but the problem is clear and underserved.**
