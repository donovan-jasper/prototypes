# JawZen

A mindfulness and wellness app that uses gentle audio reminders and tracking to help users reduce physical tension, stress, and develop healthier habits throughout their day.

**Who it's for:** Stressed professionals aged 25-45 who grind teeth or clench jaws due to work pressure, people with TMJ issues, remote workers who sit at desks all day, and wellness-conscious individuals seeking habit-forming tools.

**Gap:** Existing meditation apps focus on dedicated session times, but this addresses micro-moments of tension throughout the day. Most wellness apps don't target specific physical tension areas like jaw/clenching with gentle, non-intrusive reminders.

**Monetization:** Freemium model - free basic reminders and tracking, premium $9.99/month for personalized voice options, advanced analytics, integration with health apps, and custom reminder scheduling. People pay for health-focused apps that show measurable results.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 9/10 | Difficulty: Medium - requires good audio UX, notification management, and habit-tracking features, but core functionality is straightforward to build.**
