# SkillShot

A mobile app that gamifies skill training for real-world physical activities (e.g., throwing, shooting, batting) with AR feedback and competitive elements.

**Who it's for:** Casual gamers, athletes, parents of young athletes, and competitive gamers (e.g., Pokémon GO, Fortnite) who want to improve real-world skills.

**Gap:** Most skill-training apps lack AR integration or gamification; existing solutions are either too generic or too niche.

**Monetization:** Freemium with premium AR features ($4.99/month) and in-app purchases for custom challenges or leaderboards.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 6/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires AR development and motion sensor expertise, but the concept is scalable.**
