# FoodSafe Mobile App Specification

A hyper-local, real-time food safety and hygiene ratings app that empowers consumers to make informed dining choices with one-tap access to verified health scores and reviews.

**Who it's for:** Health-conscious consumers, parents with young children, frequent travelers, and anyone concerned about food safety in unfamiliar areas.

**Gap:** Existing apps either lack real-time hygiene data or require excessive navigation. FoodSafe would aggregate and simplify this information for instant mobile access.

**Monetization:** Freemium model with a premium tier ($4.99/month) for advanced filters (e.g., allergy-friendly options, recent inspection history).

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 8/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires partnerships with health departments and restaurant data aggregation, but the demand is clear.**
