# SketchSync

A mobile app that combines Excalidraw's whiteboard-style drawing with collaborative features and AI-powered diagram generation for quick idea visualization and team brainstorming.

**Who it's for:** Students, remote workers, project managers, and creatives who need to brainstorm, plan, or visualize ideas quickly without switching apps. Also, teams collaborating on diagrams in meetings.

**Gap:** Existing apps lack the simplicity of Excalidraw's hand-drawn feel or AI-assisted diagram generation. Mobile versions often lack offline support or real-time collaboration.

**Monetization:** Freemium with a $5/month tier for advanced AI features, team collaboration, and cloud storage. One-time $20 for lifetime access to core features.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 8/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires balancing UX for touch drawing, real-time sync, and AI integration.**
