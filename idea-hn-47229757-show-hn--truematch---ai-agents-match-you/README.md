# BehaviorMatch

A mobile app that uses AI agents to match people based on observed behavior and real-time interactions, rather than self-reported profiles, for dating, networking, and community building.

**Who it's for:** Young professionals (25-40), dating app users, and networking app users who want more authentic matches. Also, anyone who values privacy and prefers behavior-based over profile-based matching.

**Gap:** Existing apps rely on self-reported data, which is often misleading or outdated. TrueMatch uses AI to observe real behavior, leading to more accurate and meaningful matches.

**Monetization:** Freemium model with premium features like advanced matching algorithms, ad-free experience, and exclusive community access. Subscription at $9.99/month for enhanced privacy and deeper insights.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 4/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires strong AI integration, privacy compliance, and seamless user experience.**
