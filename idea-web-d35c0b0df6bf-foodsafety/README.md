# FoodGuard

A hyper-local, real-time safety and quality ratings app for restaurants, grocers, and food services, combining government data with user reviews and AI-powered alerts for recalls or violations.

**Who it's for:** Parents with young children, health-conscious adults, frequent travelers, and food delivery users who want to verify safety before ordering.

**Gap:** Most competitors either lack real-time updates, don’t aggregate multiple data sources, or are buried in non-food sections of apps.

**Monetization:** Freemium with a $4.99/month premium tier for advanced filters (e.g., allergy alerts, recall notifications, and offline maps).

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 8/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires reliable data partnerships and UI/UX to stand out in a crowded space.**
