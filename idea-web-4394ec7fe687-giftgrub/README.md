# GiftSwift

A mobile app that simplifies gifting experiences beyond food, offering curated digital or physical experiences (e.g., spa vouchers, concert tickets, or personalized messages) with seamless delivery and payment.

**Who it's for:** Busy professionals (25-45), event planners, and social media influencers who want to send thoughtful, stress-free gifts to clients, friends, or followers.

**Gap:** Existing apps either focus only on food or lack personalization. GiftGrub could combine convenience with customization (e.g., pre-packaged surprise boxes, themed gift baskets).

**Monetization:** Freemium model: Free basic gifting (e.g., food orders) with premium features like curated experiences ($5-$20 per gift) or a subscription for bulk senders ($10/month).

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 5/10 | Difficulty: Medium — Requires partnerships with delivery services and merchants, but the core concept is underserved.**
