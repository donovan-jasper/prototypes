# CrediGen

A mobile-first AI-powered content creation assistant that helps non-technical users generate images, text, and creative assets while maintaining proper attribution and credit tracking for human creators

**Who it's for:** Social media creators, small business owners, students, hobbyists, and anyone creating content for personal/professional use who wants AI assistance without technical complexity

**Gap:** Existing apps focus on pure generation but lack proper creator attribution systems and ethical frameworks. No mobile app effectively balances AI power with human creator recognition and credit management

**Monetization:** Freemium model - free basic generations, $9.99/month for unlimited high-quality outputs and advanced attribution features. Users pay for premium because they need professional-grade content with proper credit tracking

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 8/10 | Difficulty: Medium - requires solid AI integration and attribution system, but building on existing AI APIs. Differentiation through ethical framework and creator credit system**
