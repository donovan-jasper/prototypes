# ReaderRite

> A premium, one-time-purchase ebook reader app that simplifies digital library management and eliminates subscription hassles for serious readers.

**Gap:** Existing readers lack a one-time purchase model with robust file management and no forced cloud sync. Many require constant internet or have bloated i

## Features

- Students** drowning in textbook PDFs who need highlights, notes, and quick search across dozens of f
- Parents** managing kids' ebook collections without handing over credit cards to Amazon
- Privacy-conscious readers** who don't want their reading habits tracked and monetized
- International users** in regions where Kindle/Apple Books have limited catalogs or require VPNs
