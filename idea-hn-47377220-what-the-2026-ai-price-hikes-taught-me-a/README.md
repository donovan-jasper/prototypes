# AI Cost Optimizer App Spec

A mobile app that helps users optimize AI model selection for their tasks, teaching them to balance cost, performance, and efficiency in real-time

**Who it's for:** Small business owners, freelancers, and developers who use AI tools but lack expertise in model optimization, as well as budget-conscious users who want to avoid overspending on AI APIs

**Gap:** Most AI tools treat models as black boxes, making users blind to inefficiencies. No app guides users in choosing cheaper, faster alternatives without sacrificing quality

**Monetization:** Freemium model with a $9.99/month Pro tier for advanced model recommendations and API cost tracking, plus a one-time $49.99 for a 'AI Efficiency Audit' feature

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 9/10 | Difficulty: Medium—requires integrating with multiple AI APIs and educating users on trade-offs**
