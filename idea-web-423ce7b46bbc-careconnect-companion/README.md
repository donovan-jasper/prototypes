# CareConnect Companion Spec

A customizable, simplified mobile interface for anyone needing easier smartphone access due to cognitive or physical limitations, beyond generic accessibility features.

**Who it's for:** Elderly users, individuals with dementia, autism spectrum disorder, or physical disabilities, caregivers, and healthcare providers managing patients with cognitive impairments.

**Gap:** Most solutions are either too generic or require additional hardware. Few focus on medical-specific needs (e.g., medication reminders) or are overly complex for non-tech users.

**Monetization:** Freemium model with premium features like medical integration ($2.99/month) or one-time $99 for families. Caregivers could pay for group management tools.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires UX expertise, medical partnerships, and compliance with ADA/health regulations.**
