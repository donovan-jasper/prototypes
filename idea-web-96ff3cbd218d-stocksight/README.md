# MarketPulse

A hyper-personalized financial news and insights app that delivers real-time market updates, tailored recommendations, and interactive analysis tools to casual investors and traders.

**Who it's for:** Young professionals (25-45), millennials, and Gen Z investors who prefer mobile-first financial tools, as well as small business owners tracking market trends.

**Gap:** Most apps focus on trading or news but lack deep personalization, interactive charts, or seamless integration with social investing features.

**Monetization:** Freemium model with a $9.99/month premium tier offering advanced analytics, exclusive reports, and ad-free experience.

**Viability: 8/10 | Competition: 4/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires strong data partnerships and UX for complex financial information.**
