# StockSight

> A hyper-personalized financial news and insights app that delivers real-time market updates, tailored recommendations, and interactive analysis tools to casual investors and traders.

**Gap:** Most apps focus on trading or news but lack deep personalization, interactive charts, or seamless integration with social investing features.

## Features

- Broadest reach:**
- Casual investors** who check their portfolio once a week and want to know "should I be worried?"
- Side hustlers** tracking stocks related to their industry (e.g., freelance designers watching Adobe,
- Parents** managing 529 college funds or retirement accounts who need plain-English explanations
