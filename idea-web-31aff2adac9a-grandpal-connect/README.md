# GrandPal Connect Spec

A mobile app that connects people across generations for meaningful, supervised interactions and shared activities, addressing loneliness and fostering intergenerational bonds.

**Who it's for:** Loneliness-prone adults (elderly, young parents, remote workers), schools/childcare providers (for structured programs), and seniors seeking social engagement.

**Gap:** Existing apps lack structured, supervised interactions and focus on generational bonding rather than just communication.

**Monetization:** Freemium model with premium features like scheduled activities, group sessions, and verified mentor badges at $5/month. Schools/childcare providers could pay for bulk access.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 7/10 | Difficulty: Medium — requires trust-building, moderation, and partnerships with schools/healthcare providers.**
