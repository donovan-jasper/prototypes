# CareerShield

A mobile career guidance platform that helps tech professionals identify future-proof skills and career paths based on AI resistance analysis and market demand trends.

**Who it's for:** Early-career software engineers, computer science students, and mid-level tech professionals (ages 22-35) who are anxious about AI displacement and seeking strategic career advice.

**Gap:** Existing platforms offer generic courses but don't provide AI impact analysis, personalized future-proofing recommendations, or real-time market intelligence about which skills will remain valuable. No mobile-first approach to career defense against automation.

**Monetization:** Freemium model - free basic career assessment and general AI impact reports, premium ($9.99/month) for personalized skill roadmaps, 1-on-1 career coaching access, and detailed market analytics about job security in different tech niches.

**Viability: 7/10 | Competition: 8/10 | Difficulty: Medium - requires building credible algorithm for AI-resistance scoring and maintaining relationships with industry experts for insights, but core career guidance app functionality is well-established.**
